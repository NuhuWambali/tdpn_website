Building a Drug Free Tanzania

We emphasizes the commitment to eradicating drug abuse and its associated challenges in Tanzania. It reflects a vision of a healthier, more productive society where individuals, especially the youth, can thrive without the negative impact of substance abuse.We underscores the importance of collective responsibility in fostering awareness, prevention, and rehabilitation efforts, aiming to secure a brighter future for current and upcoming generations

We aim to eradicate drug abuse in Tanzania, promoting awareness, prevention, and rehabilitation for a healthier, brighter future.

What we are doing

combating drug abuse and its impact in Tanzania

Awareness Campaigns

TDPN organizes community outreach programs to educate the public about the dangers of drug abuse.

Advocacy and Policy Support

The network advocates for stronger policies and laws to prevent drug abuse, while collaborating with stakeholders

Rehabilitation and Support Services

TDPN provides support to individuals struggling with substance abuse by connecting them with rehabilitation centers.

About our organisation

We Are In A Mission To Help Helpless

At Tanzania Drugs Prevention Network (TDPN), we are on a mission to support
the vulnerable and fight against the devastating effects of drug abuse.

we strive to create a healthier, drug-free Tanzania and bring hope to those in need..
